Research

How to not use Chrome while researching:

Say you begin a in-depth study on the Barton Creek Salamandar. If you enter this topic into Chrome's search bar, go off of the information the search engine presents you on the right-hand side of the page... or not.

Instead...

Don't change your screen! Keep it at the spot it was after you pressed enter to see the results for "Barton Creek Salamandar". Scan through the list of links from Chrome and select a few that appear would be most helpful and reliable, then continue on your way.
