Skaa

The name of species may sound powerful... but they are actually the slaves.

Skaa are a species built for work. Every day, most of them are forced by the Lords to mine for metal. They have no amount of nobility and are treated like crap. They get regular beatdowns for stopping work for a minute and are always covered in black dust from the ashfalls.
